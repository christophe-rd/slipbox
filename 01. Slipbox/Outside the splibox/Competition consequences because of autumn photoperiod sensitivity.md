#### **Relevant notes**:
Compared to low-latitude ecosystems, high latitude will get the largest increases in temperature over the next century. Photoperiod will remain the same. These asynchronous changes in meteorological factors may affect plant communities and their role in carbon sequestration. 
- E.g. southerly species migrate north in response to higher temperature, these will lengthen their photosynthetic period while native, photoperiod-dependant northerly species may show less flexibility in the timing of leaf senescence, affecting competitive dynamics within plant communities.Â
