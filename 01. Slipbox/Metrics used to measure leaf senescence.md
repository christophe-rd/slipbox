#### **Relevant notes**:
List of [[Phenological metrics|metrics]] used to measure leaf senescence:
- 50% of leaf fall (the most popular)
- 100% leaf fall
- NDVI (Normalized difference vegetation index)
- 80% leaf fall
- 50% green
- 50% colour change
- 0% green
- 50% yellow.
