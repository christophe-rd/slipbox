#### **Relevant notes**:
The main nutrients withdrawn from senescent leaves are N, P, K and S, the macronutrients that are mobile in the phloem, whereas Ca and Mg, the other two macronutrients, are less mobile and are often accreted rather than resorbed at the end of the process.
